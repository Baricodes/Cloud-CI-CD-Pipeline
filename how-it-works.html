How it works

We keep the pipeline simple: you tell us where to send mail, we produce and ship it on a schedule you choose.

  1. Choose a plan. Monthly or quarterly bundles of postcards and short-form letters.
  2. Confirm your address. We validate deliverability so pieces don’t get lost along the way.
  3. We print and post. Each batch is assembled, stamped, and handed to the carrier.
  4. You open the box. Physical mail arrives on its own timeline—no inbox, no notifications.
