Pricing

Three tiers for different appetites for paper. All plans include domestic postage and recyclable materials.

Postcard drift — $8 / month

One illustrated postcard per month. Good for a light touch of mail without clutter.

Letter & line — $18 / month

A folded letter plus a postcard, monthly. Our most popular option for readers and pen-pal energy.

Parcel & post — $42 / quarter

A small mailed bundle every three months: letter, postcard, and a flat extra (bookmark, mini print, or similar).

  • Pause or skip a cycle anytime (handled at signup preferences).
  • International delivery available at adjusted rates.
